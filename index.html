Hiring: Research & Development Engineers

Red King Labs is looking for highly motivated engineers and researchers to work on cutting-edge tools and architectures across the hardware–software stack. This is a hands-on R&D role focused on building real systems and publishing high-impact research.

Role Focus Areas Expected Skills
Research Engineer – Computer Architecture Microarchitecture modeling, speculative execution analysis, cache & memory hierarchy research, performance benchmarking C/C++, Linux internals, x86/RISC-V, simulators (gem5, QEMU), performance counters, microbenchmarks
Research Engineer – Compilers Compiler optimizations, security-aware compilation, hardware-aware code generation LLVM/MLIR, GCC internals, Rust/Zig, compiler passes, IR transformations
Systems & Tooling Engineer Performance analysis tools, security instrumentation, low-level debugging and profiling Linux kernel/user-space tooling, eBPF, perf, system tracing, C/Rust
Cross-Layer Security Engineer Microarchitectural side channels, compiler-assisted mitigations, ISA–software co-design Side-channel attacks, Spectre/Meltdown class issues, compiler & hardware interaction, formal reasoning (basic)

Who Should Apply

  • Strong systems engineers with research mindset
  • Industry professionals interested in academic-quality research
  • Independent researchers and serious open-source contributors
  • Master’s / Bachelor’s graduates with proven low-level expertise

Who Should NOT Apply

  • PhD holders

  • Currently enrolled PhD candidates

  • Deep involvement in real research problems

  • Strong mentorship in architecture & compiler research
  • Flexible, research-driven work culture
